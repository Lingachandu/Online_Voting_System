Privacy Policy

1. Information We Collect

We collect your registered mobile number, full name (first name) entered during login, and your state-specific Aadhaar number format strictly to verify eligibility and authenticate your login session. We do not store plaintext Aadhaar numbers beyond registration confirmation.

2. Multi-Factor Authentication

We use Google Authenticator TOTP secrets to generate one-time passwords for secure session access. This information is securely hashed on our servers and never shared with third parties.

3. Vote Anonymity & Decoupling

We enforce strict ballot secrecy. Your candidate selection is decoupled from your voter profile and stored in separate database tables. Nobody (including administrators) can map your identity to the candidates you voted for.

4. Security of Data

All authentication records, active sessions, and database structures are stored securely using industry-standard protection mechanisms. Downloadable receipts are generated securely on-demand.

5. Cookies

We use session cookies strictly to keep you logged in while you interact with the dashboards. These cookies are destroyed once you log out or close your browser.
