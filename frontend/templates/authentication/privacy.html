Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 10, 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect your registered mobile number and your selected role (Voter/Admin) strictly for authentication and authorization purposes. No other personal data is collected or stored.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your mobile number is used to send One-Time Passwords (OTPs) to verify your identity. Your role dictates whether you access the voter dashboard or the admin audit dashboard.

3. Vote Anonymity

We take voter privacy very seriously. In this system, your cast vote is stored separately and anonymously from your user profile. Nobody can map your identity to the candidate you voted for.

4. Security of Data

All authentication records, active sessions, and data are stored securely using industry-standard database protection mechanisms. OTPs are designed to expire immediately after use or after a short validity window.

5. Cookies

We use session cookies strictly to keep you logged in while you interact with the dashboards. These cookies are destroyed once you log out or close your browser.
