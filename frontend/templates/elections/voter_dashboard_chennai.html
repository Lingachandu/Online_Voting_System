About Secure Voting (Chennai)

Welcome to the Chennai Secure Online Voting Portal, a specialized state platform designed to facilitate secure, transparent, and accessible democratic choices.

Key Security Pillars:

  • Aadhaar-Linked Validation: Voter profiles are strictly matched with registered 12-digit Aadhaar credentials on this portal starting with "789".
  • One-Person, One-Vote: Enforced through encrypted cellular OTP verification linked directly to registered profile records.
  • Anonymity Guaranteed: Votes are structurally decoupled from voter identity records to ensure absolute choice privacy.
