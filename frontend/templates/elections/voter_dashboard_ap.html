About Secure Voting (AP)

Welcome to the Andhra Pradesh Secure Online Voting Portal, a specialized state platform designed to facilitate secure, transparent, and accessible democratic choices.

Key Security Pillars:

  • Aadhaar-Linked Validation: Voter profiles are strictly matched with registered 12-digit Aadhaar credentials on this portal.
  • One-Person, One-Vote: Enforced through encrypted cellular OTP verification linked directly to registered profile records.
  • Anonymity Guaranteed: Votes are structurally decoupled from voter identity records to ensure absolute choice privacy.

Ensure that you keep your registered mobile device safe and never share OTP codes with third parties.
